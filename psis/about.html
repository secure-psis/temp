About Us

With our reliable and innovative solutions, we help our clients our meet thier requirements with utmost safety of their data and privacy.

Pie Star Interactive Studios was founded by Vikas Sharma and Parv Sharma, two ambitious college students who met on their first day of college. From the start, they knew they wanted to do something new and challenging, something that would change the world through technology. In their first year of college, Vikas and Parv founded Pie Star Interactive Studios, a company that specializes in creating innovative and engaging digital experiences. The company quickly gained attention for its unique approach to technology and its commitment to excellence. Today, Pie Star Interactive Studios is in its second year of operation and has already made a name for itself in the industry. The team has worked on a variety of projects, from mobile apps and games to websites and virtual reality experiences. Despite being a relatively new company, Pie Star Interactive Studios has already built a reputation for delivering high-quality work that exceeds clients' expectations. Vikas and Parv attribute this success to their team's creativity, dedication, and passion for technology. Looking ahead, the team at Pie Star Interactive Studios is excited to continue pushing the boundaries of what's possible with technology. They are committed to staying at the forefront of innovation and to creating digital experiences that are both engaging and impactful. If you're looking for a team that can help you bring your digital vision to life, look no further than Pie Star Interactive Studios.
